1. Introduction

Welcome to Luma Notes ("we," "our," "us," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

This Privacy Policy explains how Luma Notes handles information when you use our mobile application available on Android devices through the Google Play Store.

By using Luma Notes, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection

Information We Do NOT Collect

Luma Notes is designed as a fully offline, privacy-first application. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Contact lists or calendar data
  • Browsing history
  • Any content you create in the app

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Notes you create (text, formatting, styles)
  • Images you attach to notes
  • Audio recordings you make within the app
  • Folders and organizational structure
  • App preferences and settings
  • Hidden vault PIN (stored encrypted)
  • Backup files you create

3. Data Storage

üè† 100% Local Storage

All data created in Luma Notes is stored exclusively on your device's local storage. We operate with:

  • No cloud servers - We don't have servers to store your data
  • No remote databases - Your notes never leave your device
  • No sync services - Data stays only where you put it
  • No network requests - The app works completely offline

Storage Location

Your data is stored in the app's private storage area on your Android device. This area is:

  • Protected by Android's app sandboxing
  • Inaccessible to other apps
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

4. App Permissions

Luma Notes requests only the minimum permissions necessary for functionality:

Permission Purpose When Used
Camera/Storage To attach photos to your notes Only when you choose to add an image
Microphone To record voice memos Only when you start a recording
Biometrics To unlock your hidden vault Only if you enable biometric unlock

Important: All permissions are optional. You can deny any permission and still use the core note-taking features of the app.

5. Data Sharing

We Do Not Share Your Data

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share. Specifically:

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share data with advertisers
  • We do not share data with analytics providers
  • We do not share data with any third parties
  • We do not use any third-party SDKs that collect data

Third-Party Services

Luma Notes does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data. The app functions entirely independently.

User-Initiated Sharing

You can choose to share your notes using Android's share feature. When you do this:

  • You control exactly what is shared
  • You choose the destination app
  • We have no involvement in the sharing process

6. Data Security

How Your Data is Protected

  • Android Security: Your data is protected by Android's built-in app sandboxing
  • Device Encryption: If your device has encryption enabled, your notes are encrypted at rest
  • Hidden Vault: Sensitive notes can be protected with an additional PIN
  • Biometric Lock: Optional fingerprint or face authentication for the vault
  • No Network Exposure: Since data never leaves your device, there's no risk of interception

Vault PIN Security

Your hidden vault PIN is:

  • Stored locally in encrypted form
  • Never transmitted anywhere
  • Not recoverable by us if forgotten

7. Data Retention & Deletion

How Long Data is Stored

Your data remains on your device until:

  • You manually delete individual notes
  • You empty the trash
  • You clear the app's data in device settings
  • You uninstall the app

Deleting Your Data

You have complete control over your data:

Action How to Do It
Delete a note Swipe or use the delete option on any note
Permanently delete Go to Trash and delete forever
Delete all data Uninstall the app or clear app data in settings
Delete backups Manually delete backup files from your device

8. Children's Privacy

Luma Notes does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction).

Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages. However, the app is intended for general audiences and not specifically designed for children.

Parents should supervise their children's device usage as with any application.

9. Your Rights

Under GDPR (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights regarding your personal data. However, since Luma Notes does not collect or process any personal data:

  • Right to Access: All your data is already accessible to you on your device
  • Right to Rectification: You can edit your notes anytime
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete any or all data at any time
  • Right to Portability: You can export/backup your data
  • Right to Object: Not applicable as we don't process data

Under CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act. Since we don't collect personal information:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • There is no personal information to disclose, delete, or correct on our end

For All Users

Regardless of your location, you have full control over your data because it exists only on your device.

10. International Users
